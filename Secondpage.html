About myself

By Yanran Huang

Visual Storyteller

I'm Yanran Huang, visual storyteller. Currently I'm a MU journalism student in Convergence Photojournalism.

"When you see the moment, you lose the moment."

It's the best description for many photographers and visual storytellers like me when we are involving in a story.

I always keep it in my mind and remind myself again and again. Pursuing the decisive moment and catching it are important to journalism.
